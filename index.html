ABOUT ME

The eye behind the lens

Hi, I'm David Ogunleye

I’m a professional photographer and visual storyteller based in Scotland, working under Justlink 90’s Visuals. I create visuals that go beyond the ordinary blending creativity, culture, and emotion to produce work that feels authentic and distinctive.

I’m a versatile creative, working across multiple styles including lifestyle, fashion, portraits, and events. Rather than limiting myself to one niche, I adapt my approach to suit each project, ensuring every visual tells a unique story.

My work is rooted in storytelling capturing moments and transforming them into powerful narratives through both photography and video. From events to music videos and creative concepts, I bring ideas to life in a way that’s fresh, cinematic, and intentionally different from the norm.
